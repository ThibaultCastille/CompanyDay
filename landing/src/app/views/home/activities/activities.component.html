COMPANY FAIR

The main meeting point of the event, a place where companies and U-tad’s talents can meet, discuss their projects and explore opportunities.

STUDENT PITCHES

Animation and VFX short films, video games, design portfolios, apps, extended realities experiences…

SPEED MEETINGS

Job and internship interviews. For companies: you will have access to a pull of CVs to filter and select the best ones before the event, in order to prepare this activity. For students: you will be able to apply for the offers and companies you are more interested in.

COMPANY TALKS

For companies: Go on stage and share your experience. Propose a topic that resonates with the kinds of candidates you are seeking for. For students: get to know what companies are looking for, meet recruiters and professionals, take advantage to obtain first-hand information.
